About TaxMate

TaxMate is a comprehensive digital solution aimed at helping vulnerable and at-risk groups, such as people from culturally diverse backgrounds, low-income individuals, older citizens, and those with low digital literacy, comply with their tax and superannuation obligations. By leveraging open government data and artificial intelligence, TaxMate offers personalized guidance, proactive notifications, and a user-friendly platform to make tax compliance easier for all.

Key Features

TaxMate provides a wide range of features designed to simplify the tax process for vulnerable groups:

  • AI-powered chatbot for 24/7 assistance in multiple languages
  • Proactive notifications and reminders for tax filing deadlines
  • Personalized educational resources in text, video, and audio formats
  • Financial health tools to assess and improve financial stability
  • Mobile-first design to ensure accessibility for all users

Data Sources

TaxMate uses open government datasets to provide valuable insights and support:

  • Taxation Statistics 2021-22
  • Self-Managed Superannuation Funds
  • Higher Education Loan Program (HELP)
  • Household Use of Information Technology
  • Census Data
