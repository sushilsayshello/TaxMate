About TaxMate

TaxMate is designed to support at-risk and vulnerable groups, such as older citizens, migrants, rural residents, and those facing financial difficulties. By leveraging open government data and AI-powered insights, we offer personalized support and guidance, ensuring users can access the benefits they deserve.

Key Features

AI-Powered Assistance

Our AI-driven chatbot provides 24/7 guidance on tax return submissions and super queries in multiple languages.

Proactive Notifications

Receive personalized reminders for tax filing deadlines, super contributions, and available benefits based on your financial situation.

Educational Hub

Access easy-to-understand resources, including video tutorials, FAQs, and step-by-step guides, designed with inclusivity in mind.

Financial Health Tools

Get personalized financial health assessments and suggestions to improve your tax and superannuation management.

TaxMate is a platform that helps Australians, particularly vulnerable and at-risk groups, with tax and superannuation compliance through personalized, AI-driven support and educational resources.

TaxMate is designed for individuals who may struggle with tax compliance, including older citizens, rural residents, migrants, and those with low digital literacy or financial hardship.

The AI-powered chatbot provides real-time support, answering your tax-related questions, guiding you through tax return submissions, and offering advice in multiple languages.
